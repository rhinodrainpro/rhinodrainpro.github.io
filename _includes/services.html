CCTV Inspection

A CCTV drain inspection allows our experts to explore entire drainage systems with state of the art cameras. Far more economical and less disruptive than digging a trench, the CCTV equipment is deployed to identify blockages and leaks, as well as to conduct drainage and asset mapping. This not only saves time but also helps keep drain repair costs to a minimum.

A CCTV inspection can uncover:
  • - Tree root intrusion
  • - Cracks or breaks in the pipe
  • - Obstructions within the pipe
  • - Crushed or collapsed areas of pipe

In-drain Root Cutting

One of the common reasons behind serious blockage in Auckland is tree roots. With time tree roots can grow expansively throughout drainage system causing heavy blockage and sometimes even damage the drain badly. To remove tree roots from your drain, Rhino drain pro uses a powerful hydro jet unit. This jet is fitted with a special root cutting nozzle allowing it to get through the toughest tree roots making sure your pipes are back in action in no time.
